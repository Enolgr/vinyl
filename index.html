The History of a Legendary Band

Gorillaz, the brainchild of Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett, broke new ground in 1998 as a virtual band with animated members and a genre-blending sound. From the start, they captivated fans with a unique fusion of hip-hop, rock, and electronic music, combined with striking visuals that redefined what a band could be.

With albums like *Demon Days* solidifying their place in music history, Gorillaz quickly became icons, inspiring countless artists and fans around the world. Known for pushing creative boundaries, they brought their animated universe to life with each release, creating a legacy that’s both musical and visual.
